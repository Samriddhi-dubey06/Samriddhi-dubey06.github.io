My Journey at IITGN Robotics Lab

I am incredibly fortunate to be part of the prestigious IITGN Robotics Lab. This lab has become my second home, where I've had the privilege to work on cutting-edge robotics research under exceptional mentorship.

The lab is co-managed by Professor Harish PM and Professor Madhu Vadali, both exceptional mentors who have guided me throughout my research journey. Their expertise and supportive approach have been instrumental in my growth as a robotics researcher.

The lab environment is truly world-class, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and equipment that enable innovative research in various domains of robotics. What makes this lab special is how we work together as a team, collaborating on projects and supporting each other's research endeavors.

Currently, I'm working on a project in collaboration with Addverb Technologies, a leading robotics company specializing in warehouse automation. In 2024 December, I had the valuable opportunity to visit their facility, which provided practical insights into industrial applications of robotics research. This collaboration has enriched my understanding of how academic research can be translated into real-world solutions.

My journey at the IITGN Robotics Lab continues to be a transformative experience. Each day brings new challenges and learning opportunities that fuel my passion for robotics.
